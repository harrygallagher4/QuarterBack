What is QuarterBack

QuarterBack is an all-new application for iOS and Android phones that allows users to keep track of their debts. The app sports PayPal and Google Wallet integration so users can easily transfer payments to others.

We've got it all.

QuarterBack is developed by regular people just like you. This means we adapt to normal people's needs, and keep the app as user-friendly as possible.


Smart

QuarterBack is smarter than ever. We'll automatically calculate net debts between you and friends, so you can tackle your debts with fewer, smaller payments.

Compatible

Integrated with the latest finance applications like PayPal and Google Wallet, QuarterBack lets you and your friends easily transfer funds instantly, with little-to-no fees.

Reliable

We'll remind you if you owe someone, and remind your friends if they owe you. We'll also keep track of any past dues or payments, in case you happen to forget.

We've done our research.

We took a quick survey before we started. Turns out, you need this just as much as you thought.

The Facts

Almost half of mobile users surveyed said they would benefit from a way to track their loaning habits.

Three in ten people loan money to their friends often, and all of those people commonly forget how much.

About half of people surveyed already use a payment service or would sign up for one if it allowed them to pay their friends back.
